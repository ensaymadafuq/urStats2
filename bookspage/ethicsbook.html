Ethics

                  Ethics is the study of moral principles that guide human behavior, helping us distinguish right from wrong and make responsible decisions. This course explores different ethical theories, real-life dilemmas, and the reasoning behind our choices, encouraging students to think critically and act with integrity. Through discussions, case studies, and reflection, students learn to apply ethical thinking in both personal and professional contexts, preparing them to navigate complex situations with fairness and accountability.
